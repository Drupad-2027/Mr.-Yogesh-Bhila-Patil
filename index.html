✨ About the Book

When I Was Me? (Based on true incidents)

“When I Was Me?” based on true incidents… This book is dedicated to my son ‘J’ and his generation for their coming future. An autobiography to reach someone out there who needs the same support he once longed for. If even one soul finds strength through his journey; then every struggle he endured would have found its true purpose. He believes life is not always about chasing the big dreams but cherishing the small blessings around us. Those simple yet precious moments with his son ‘J’ became the foundation of his strength.

He wants to share one message with the world — ‘Acceptance and Moving Forward.’ Life may leave many questions unanswered; but the secret is to focus on what lies ahead rather than what is left behind. Through this book, he wishes to inspire those who have fallen to rise again and rewrite their destiny. He has navigated through the highs and lows of life with resilience and courage. He believes that life always gives a second chance to those who refuse to quit, no matter how hard life knocks them down.

The writing style is clear and uncomplex, simple and engaging — making it highly resonating and connecting to the audience. Also, this book is full of knowledge and information.

“If you ever find yourself wondering, ‘When I was me?’ know that you are not alone!”
